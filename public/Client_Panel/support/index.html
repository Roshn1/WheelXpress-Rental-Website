Frequently Asked Questions

How can I rent a two-wheeler online? +

You can rent a two-wheeler by visiting our website, selecting a vehicle, and completing the booking process.

Do I need to pay a security deposit to rent a vehicle? +

Yes, a security deposit is required based on the vehicle type.

What is the condition of the vehicles? +

We ensure that our vehicles are well-maintained and in perfect condition.

Is home delivery possible for the vehicle? +

Yes, home delivery is available for certain locations.

What documents are required for renting a scooter/bike ? +

We have a digital KYC process that takes less than 60 seconds to complete.Just upload your Driving License, Aadhar, and Local Address Proof and you are good to go!

How do I get booking confirmation? How will I know that my request has been accpeted? +

Once the payment is done successfully, you’ll receive the booking confirmation and details to your registered mobile number. You can also check the same in your account under booking details section.

How can I cancel my booking and what are the cancellation charges? +

The cancellations request received before 24hrs will be cancelled without any deductions. For more details on the cancellations please read our cancellation policy.

What if I dont get a vehicle after booking ? +

Since this is a limited facility and is on First Come First Serve basis, we thus cannot ensure the vehicle to everyone with utmost certainty. We however will try to satisfy as much as request as we can. If for any reasons the vehicle is not available, we will inform you the same. In this case no deductions will be carried out. Moreover, you will be informed at the latest when we have it available for you.

Can I extend my current booking? +

If you wish to continue your facility you can contact us post which we will extend the facility tenure for you. This is however subjected to any other pending request received to us for availing the service. The decision solely lies with Team Freedo in this regard.

Is there any distance limit on the vehicles ? What if I cross the distance limit ? +

Yes, all the vehicles come with distance (km) capping with them which are as per the package selected by you. After exhaustion of the distance limit the extra Km over the limit specified will be deducted at ₹2/km.

Do you provide any assistance if the vehicle breaks down ? +

Inform us at the earliest and we will assist you further. Any deductions of the repair will be on you against genuine spare parts. However, any wear and tear damages from natural causes will be levied.

What if I get caught violating traffic rules ? +

Traffic rules are not only meant for your safety but for others in road too. Thus as mentioned we encourage you to ride responsibly but in case such incident happens you bear the full responsibility to pay any penalty to the concerned authorities.

Is there any penalty on late return? +

We encourage you to always extend your facility in advance by informing us. Post which we will share a confirmation mail to you. However, if you are late minor deductions will be carried out proportional to the package chosen by you.

My vehicle is stolen during the period when I availed it ? +

In case of such mishaps we request you to inform us at the earliest. Post which it is advised to lodge an FIR with the nearest police station. You shall need to be available in all investigations as required by law. So we recommend you take care of the vehicle till it's yours.
